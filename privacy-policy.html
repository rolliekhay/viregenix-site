We use the information you provide exclusively to:

  • Perform the full quantitative Portfolio Optimization Review you paid for, across all 8 analytical sections

  • Communicate with you about your engagement, scheduling, and findings
  • Send follow-up information directly related to your engagement
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your personal or financial data with any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your portfolio data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality.
