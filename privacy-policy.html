Privacy Policy

Effective: May 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Viregenix LLC, a company registered under the laws of the State of Wyoming, United States, operating the Quantitative Real Estate Advisory services of Rollie Khay ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our services or visit our website.

For privacy-related questions contact us at support@viregenix.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you book a Portfolio Diagnostic Call or complete our intake form, we collect:

  • Personal identification: Full name, email address, profession
  • Financial portfolio data: Property addresses or nicknames, property values, purchase prices, rental income, operating expenses, mortgage balances, interest rates, debt structure, occupancy status
  • Investment information: Goals, risk tolerance, decision-making processes, portfolio concerns
  • Payment information: Processed securely through our payment provider. We do not store card numbers on our systems.
  • Communication data: Emails and messages you send us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively to:

  • Perform the quantitative portfolio analysis you paid for
  • Prepare your personalized diagnostic before your call
  • Communicate with you about your session and findings
  • Send follow-up information directly related to your engagement
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your personal or financial data with any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your portfolio data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. We store your intake form responses and analysis in password-protected, encrypted environments accessible solely to Rollie Khay for the purpose of performing your diagnostic. We retain your data for a maximum of 12 months following your session, after which it is permanently deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Payment processing: Stripe or equivalent
  • Scheduling: Calendly or equivalent
  • Form collection: Typeform or Tally
  • Email communication: Standard email provider

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time. To exercise these rights contact support@viregenix.com.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

8. International Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation. Our lawful basis for processing your data is the performance of the contract you entered into when booking our service.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to active clients. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions contact Viregenix LLC at support@viregenix.com.
