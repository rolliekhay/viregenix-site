Part E

Risk & Awareness

These are the 12 core diagnostic questions. Your answers — combined with the property data from Part C — form the foundation of all 8 sections of your Portfolio Optimization Review. Be as honest and specific as possible.

Question 01 · Portfolio-level IRR * "Have you ever calculated your portfolio-level IRR — meaning the return across all properties combined, not individual deal returns?"
Question 02 · Correlation between properties * "Have you calculated the correlation between your properties — meaning do they tend to go up and down together or independently of each other?"
Question 03 · Sharpe ratio * "Have you calculated the Sharpe ratio of your real estate portfolio — your return per unit of risk?"
Question 04 · Interest rate sensitivity * "If interest rates rose 300 basis points tomorrow, what happens to your debt service coverage across every property?"
Question 05 · Tenant profile * "How would you describe the tenant profile across your properties — are they similar types of people or quite different?"
Question 06 · Current vacancy rate * "What is your current average vacancy rate across your entire portfolio?"
Question 07 · Monte Carlo simulation * "Have you ever run a Monte Carlo simulation on your portfolio — modeling thousands of possible return scenarios to understand your true risk range?"
Question 08 · Efficient frontier * "Do you know where your portfolio sits on the efficient frontier — the curve showing the maximum possible return for each level of risk?"
Question 09 · Recession stress test * "Have you stress tested your portfolio against a scenario where vacancy rises 15% and interest rates spike 200 basis points at the same time?"
Question 10 · Geographic concentration * "What percentage of your total portfolio value is concentrated in your single largest market?"
Question 11 · Self-assessed diversification * "On a scale of 1 to 10, how confident are you that your portfolio is genuinely diversified — not just spread across multiple properties, but mathematically independent in how the properties perform?"
